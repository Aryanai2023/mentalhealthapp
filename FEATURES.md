## üå¨Ô∏è 7. Box Breathing Exercise

**What it does:**
- Guided breathing exercise with visual animation
- 4-4-4-4 pattern (inhale-hold-exhale-hold)
- Tracks number of breathing cycles
- Start/stop controls

**Why it's helpful:**
- Reduces anxiety and stress immediately
- Calms the nervous system
- Improves focus and clarity
- Can be done anywhere, anytime

**The Pattern:**
1. Inhale for 4 seconds
2. Hold for 4 seconds
3. Exhale for 4 seconds
4. Hold for 4 seconds
5. Repeat

**When to use:**
- Feeling anxious or overwhelmed
- Before important events
- When having trouble sleeping
- During panic attacks
- To center yourself
